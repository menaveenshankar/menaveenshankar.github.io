Hello Random Walker!

I am a Machine Learning engineer, currently working on 3D perception at BMW Group. Previously, I was involved in deploying the first data driven component viz. Lane Change Prediction in series BMW cars (release 2018). Before joining BMW, I spent some wonderful years in Freiburg working on Video Segmentation, supervised by Prof. Thomas Brox.

I’m interested in all aspects of Augmented Intelligence, especially using minimal data. Apart from being a developer in the autonomous driving project, I also supervise students on exciting topics.
